About Me

I'm a 20-year-old Python programmer from Kampala, Uganda. I have a strong interest in Geospatial Studies and Web Development.

I'm currently studying Geographical Information Systems (GIS), learning to apply AI and Machine Learning techniques in Satellite Data Analysis for agriculture, at the Quantum School of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (QSAIR), founded by Robert Steven Owor — a professor of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Albany State University in Georgia, USA.

Outside of coding, I love playing basketball, American football, and chess. You'll also catch me running long distances. I'm an avid enjoyer of watching Formula 1, playing video games, and listening to music.

I'm always open to collaborating on projects involving Python, GIS, and front-end web development.
