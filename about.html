About Me

I am a 20-year-old Python developer based in Kampala, Uganda, with a strong interest in Geospatial Studies and Web Development.

I am currently studying Geographical Information Systems (GIS), with a focus on applying Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques to satellite data analysis for agricultural applications. I am undertaking this training at the Quantum School of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (QSAIR), founded by Robert Steven Owor, a Professor of Computer Science and Software Engineering at Albany State University.

Beyond technology, I am actively engaged in sports and personal development. I enjoy playing basketball, running, and competing in chess. I am also an enthusiastic follower of Formula 1, a gamer, and a dedicated music listener.

I am open to collaborative opportunities involving Python development, GIS applications, and front-end web technologies.
